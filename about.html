about us

our mission

We started the reel project because we believe small businesses deserve access to creative branding and digital marketing too.

Our goal is to help local entrepreneurs build an online presence that feels authentic to them and connects them with their community.

why we do it

Big companies have entire teams dedicated to branding, photography, content, and social media.

We're here to make those creative resources more accessible to smaller businesses while learning, creating, and growing alongside the people we work with.

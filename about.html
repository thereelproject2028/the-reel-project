our story

Hi, we're the Reel Project team! We're a group of high schoolers with a shared love for design and digital marketing. We started this because we wanted small businesses in our community to have access to the same polished branding and creative support that big companies can afford.

our mission

Our goal is to help local entrepreneurs gain visibility, connect with customers, and build brands that genuinely represent who they are.
